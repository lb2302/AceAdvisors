Frequent Questions

What documents do I need to apply for a mortgage?
Typically, you will need your Passport/Visa copies, Emirates ID, Salary Certificate (or Trade License if self-employed), and 6 months of bank statements. We help you organize this entire package.
How much down payment is required?
For UAE expats, the minimum down payment is usually 20% for a first property under AED 5 million. For UAE Nationals, it is 15%. Off-plan properties may have different payment plans.
What is the difference between Fixed and Variable rates?
A Fixed Rate stays the same for a set period (e.g., 3 years), giving you payment certainty. A Variable Rate is linked to EIBOR and can go up or down depending on market conditions.
Can I refinance my existing mortgage?
Absolutely. If interest rates have dropped or your property value has increased, refinancing can lower your monthly payments or allow you to release equity (cash out) for other investments.
