Frequent Questions

What documents do I need to apply for a mortgage?
Typically, you will need your Passport/Visa copies, Emirates ID, Salary Certificate (or Trade License if self-employed), and 6 months of bank statements.
How much down payment is required?
For UAE expats, the minimum down payment is usually 20% for a first property under AED 5 million. For UAE Nationals, it is 15%.
What is the difference between Fixed and Variable rates?
A Fixed Rate stays the same for a set period (e.g., 3 years), giving you payment certainty. A Variable Rate is linked to EIBOR and can fluctuate.
